A 'study buddy' for family medicine curriculums

Written by physicians, the Family Medicine Study Guide is a portable curriculum that includes 50 original case studies and over 250 questions to help motivate, guide and assist you in your studying.  

What makes this app special?

Learn through application

Rather than reading about a problem, with the Family Medicine Study Guide, you learn by doing. Each case tests you on key points on diagnosing and managing common conditions.

'Clinical pearls' emphasize key learning points

Each case includes a few Clinical Pearls that help commit key information to memory. Spend less time on minutiae, and more time focusing on the high-yield stuff.

Test your knowledge with 'rapid fire' questions

Short on time? Rapid-fire questions quiz you on random topics in Family Medicine. They're a great way to improve spontaneous recall.
